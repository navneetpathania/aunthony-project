About Remittance

Remittance is people sending money home to people they love. Remittance is the largest source of foriegn aid. It far outstrips UN aid in terms of impact to people in developing countries. qwallet and stellar were made to help people who are unbanked do banking more efficiently. As a part of that we have several apps that can help you send more to people you love.
